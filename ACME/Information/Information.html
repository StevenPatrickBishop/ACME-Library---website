Resources

Group projects

We have 2 large and 3 small group study rooms with large screen computers available for groups of 2-16 people on a first come first served basis!

Research

Media center assistants have created online course specific research guides. These guides list the best sources, both print and online, available for a topic or research assignment. They also contain video tutorials, citation toolkits, and address FAQs about the library.

Rules

Cheking out items

  • Students may borrow up to 20 items
  • Guests may borrow up to 10 items
  • Books and Government Documents are loaned in 3 week intervals with 2 extension periods allowed
  • CDs and Audio Books are loaned in 3 week intervals with 2 extension periods allowed (limit 5)
  • DVDs are loaned for 7 days with no extension periods allowed (limit 2)
  • Journals, magazines and reference books are in library only
  • Supplementary materials placed by faculty in the library for student use are located at the front desk for in-library use only
  • Books or periodical articles not available in this library may be requested through the Interlibrary loan. This service is available to student and staff only.

Food and Drinks

  • Food and drinks are allowed in the café and lounge only

Cell phones

  • Cell phones are to not to be used in the library and must be turn off or volume turned down
